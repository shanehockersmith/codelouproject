About the band

Giant Moose began as an abstract concept in the mind of Mars Marson back in 1995. On a crisp autumn day at the age of only 4 Mars decided to create the greatest band of all time. All of Mars' time has been about creating Giant Moose.
In March of 2011 Mars finally found the rest of his band mates; 'Snake' Peters, John 'Third-eye' Morales, and Kenneth Smith.
The band recorded their first EP in June of 2011 titled 'This is a Man, A Robot Man' The EP went on to sell over 40,000 copies in 23 days.
Giant Moose signed with Blah Blah Records by the end of the year. The band started working on their debut album, 'Heavy Hitter with the Large Antlers and a Gun'
